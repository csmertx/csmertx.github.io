## KDE (MTP)

- ```pac -S kio-extras```

- ```pac -S android-tools```
    
    > Or turn off ADB in Android device settings

- ```apti android-tools-adb kio-extras```

    > 05-29-22: No longer a solution w/Kubuntu 22.04?

## Gnome (MTP)

- ```pac -S gvfs-mtp```

- ```pac -S android-tools```

    > Or turn off ADB in Android device settings
